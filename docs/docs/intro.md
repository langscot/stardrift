# What is Stellar Drift?

**Stellar Drift** is a persistent space MMO played entirely inside Discord. No launcher, no client download — just slash commands and buttons in your server.

The universe is a shared galaxy of over **100,000 star systems**. Each Discord server that joins becomes one of those systems. You mine asteroids, haul cargo across light-years of space, undercut rivals at the market, and eventually build a corporation that spans the galaxy.

---

## Core Concept

> **One Discord server = one solar system.**

When your server enrolls in Stellar Drift, it claims a system from the galaxy and becomes a live node in the shared universe. Other players can warp in, trade at your station, or just pass through on their way somewhere else.

The game's home server hosts **Sol Nexus** — the central hub at galactic coordinates (0, 0) where every new pilot spawns.

---

## How it Works

All gameplay happens through Discord interactions:

- **Slash commands** — `/mine`, `/sell`, `/travel`, `/map`, and more
- **Buttons** — confirm a jump, sell your cargo, mine again with one click
- **Location-based channels** — each system has dedicated channels for the station market, planets, asteroid belts, and a travel hub. You can only interact with a system's channels when your ship is docked there.
