# Roadmap

Stardrift launched as a focused MVP. Here's where the game is headed.

---

## Currently Live (MVP)

- ✅ **System Enrollment** — Discord servers claim systems in the shared galaxy
- ✅ **Mining** — Ore extraction from planets and asteroid belts
- ✅ **Inventory** — Cargo hold and station storage
- ✅ **NPC Market** — Dynamic pricing with supply-driven decay
- ✅ **Travel** — Real-time jumps between systems with fuel and time costs
- ✅ **Star Map** — Local galaxy view with nearby system info
- ✅ **Proxy Systems** — Mirror systems across multiple servers
- ✅ **100,000+ System Galaxy** — Pre-generated persistent universe

---

## Phase 2 — Economy & Ships

Player-driven markets and a proper ship system are the next major additions.

### Player Market
Replace NPC-only trading with a full order-book market:
- Post **buy orders** (offer credits for ore)
- Post **sell orders** (list ore at an asking price)
- Orders persist until filled or cancelled
- Cross-system market browsing

### Ship Classes
Multiple ship hulls with different stats:

| Ship | Role | Cargo | Fuel Tank | Speed |
|------|------|-------|-----------|-------|
| Shuttle | Starter | 1,000 | 100 | Standard |
| Mining Barge | Mining | 5,000 | 200 | Slow |
| Freighter | Hauling | 20,000 | 500 | Slow |
| Explorer | Scouting | 500 | 1,000 | Fast |
| Corvette | Combat | 200 | 300 | Fast |
| Cruiser | Combat/Fleet | 1,000 | 600 | Standard |

### Ship Modules
Equip modules to customize your ship:
- Expanded cargo bays
- Mining laser upgrades (yield bonuses)
- Fuel efficiency drives
- Warp speed enhancers

---

## Phase 3 — Progression & Skills

### Skill System
Train skills over real time to unlock bonuses:
- **Mining skills** — yield multipliers, cooldown reduction
- **Trade skills** — better sell prices, market analysis
- **Navigation skills** — fuel efficiency, travel speed
- **Engineering** — module fitting bonuses

### XP & Levels
Earn XP from all activities. Level up to unlock higher-tier ships, modules, and skills.

---

## Phase 4 — Corporations

### Corps (Guilds)
Form a corporation with other players:
- Shared **corporate treasury**
- Member roles (CEO, Director, Member)
- **Group buffs** when corp members are in the same system
- Coordinated fleet travel

### Population Buffs
Systems with more active players get bonuses:
- Mining yield boosts
- Market price improvements
- Defense bonuses (for future combat)

---

## Phase 5 — Exploration

### System Discovery
Unenrolled systems can be **scanned** to reveal their stats before committing to enrollment:
- Spend fuel to probe a system
- Preview planet types and resource rating
- **Bank** promising discoveries for 72 hours — a claim window before others can grab it

---

## Phase 6 — Combat

### PvP
Player-vs-player combat zones in contested space:
- Initiate attacks on ships in the same system
- Destroy or disable enemy ships to loot their cargo
- **Safe zones** around hub systems protect new players

### Corporation Wars
Formalize conflicts between corps:
- Declare war through an in-game interface
- War systems become combat zones
- Victory conditions and spoils

### System Ownership
System owners gain governance control:
- Set custom **docking fees** for visitors
- Configure **mining taxes** on all ore extracted in their system
- Set **docking permissions** (whitelist/blacklist corps)
- Capture systems by defeating their defenders

---

## Phase 7 — Prestige

Long-term replayability for veteran players:
- Prestige reset option — start fresh with permanent bonuses
- Galaxy-wide leaderboards (credits, ore mined, systems owned)
- Seasonal events with limited-time resources and rewards
