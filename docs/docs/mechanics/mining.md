# Mining

Mining is the primary way to earn credits early on. Use `/mine` in any **planet channel** or **asteroid belt channel** to extract ore from that body.

## How It Works

Each `/mine` command rolls a random amount of ore based on the planet type or asteroid belt richness. The result goes directly into your **cargo hold**. If your cargo is full, mining is blocked until you sell or jettison cargo.

Mining has a **30-second cooldown** between actions. After a successful mine, a **Mine Again** button appears — click it for a quick re-queue without retyping the command.

---

## Planet Types & Ore Tables

Different planet types yield different resources. Rarer ores come from harder-to-reach or more hostile worlds.

| Planet Type | Common Resources | Rare Resources |
|-------------|-----------------|----------------|
| **Rocky** | Iron, Copper, Silicon | Titanium |
| **Scorched** | Iron, Titanium, Platinum | Crystal Ore |
| **Temperate** | Iron, Copper, Silicon | — |
| **Gas Giant** | Helium Gas, Hydrogen Gas | — |
| **Ice World** | Ice Crystal, Silicon | Hydrogen Gas |
| **Barren** | Iron, Silicon, Copper | — |
| **Volcanic** | Titanium, Platinum, Crystal Ore | Dark Matter |

Volcanic worlds are the richest sources of high-value ore. Barren worlds exist in most systems but yield little of value.

---

## Asteroid Belts

Every system may include one or more asteroid belts. Belts have a **richness rating from 1–5** — higher richness multiplies ore yield. Belt ore types include Iron, Copper, Silicon, Titanium, and Platinum.

## Proxy System Penalty

If you're mining in a **proxy system** (a system mirrored from another guild), your yield is reduced to **70%** of normal. Proxy systems are visually marked with a 📡 prefix on their category name.

For maximum yield, travel to the system's home guild server.

---

## Tips

- **Volcanic planets** offer the best ore variety and value, but they're rarer.
- **Asteroid belt richness** matters — a belt rated 5 yields significantly more than a belt rated 1.
- Keep an eye on your cargo capacity with `/inventory`. A full hold wastes cooldown ticks.
- Sell frequently if you're in a busy market; prices decay as more ore floods in.
